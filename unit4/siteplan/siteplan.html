Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to create a shopping catalog page that can mimic ones found in real life, and allow people to go through and see what clothing might interest them.

Audience

This website will be tailored to adults 16 and up who want to shop for cheap clothing. It will provide a somewhat modern website design that will attempt to be easy to navigate.

Dynamic Elements

There will be a dynamic sorting menu on the left side of all the products filled with a price range, certain tags, or other elements that will provide as a mechanism to find what's needed.
